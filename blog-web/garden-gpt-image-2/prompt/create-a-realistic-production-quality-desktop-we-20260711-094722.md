CONCEPT: "Light Novel Reading Desk". The first article section must peek into the initial viewport.

SECONDARY CONTENT: use a narrow left margin for chapter markers, reading progress, or small decorative footnotes. A minimal author note can appear near the article ending. Avoid a conventional boxed sidebar and avoid excessive cards.

REFERENCE RELATIONSHIP: only borrow the idea of a full-width anime header leading into a long article-driven homepage. The final layout must be clearly original and more editorial, with different hierarchy, no copied bordered article cards, no copied sidebar stack, no copied mascot, and no copied decorative wave.
